About

A bit about me

I'm a developer who learns by building real, shipped products, not just tutorials. My favorite project so far is lovfamilia, a calm, private app for staying close to the people you love without the pressure of social media, it's where I discovered how much I care about designing technology that actually improves people's lives. Alongside it, I've built Prompt Firewall, an AI security API that screens chatbot messages for attacks, a full multi-page website for a ministry (Icing Family), and a complete e-commerce storefront (Informal Trade Hub). Each project used a different stack and pushed me to adapt quickly, and I'm comfortable moving between front-end, back-end, and everything in between. I bring genuine curiosity, adaptability, and care to every project I take on, and I'm looking for a team where I can keep building things that matter.
